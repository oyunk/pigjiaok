evaporation float This float🛶 helps optimize solar🌞 evaporation by evaporating only the surface layer of the water of the solar still ! It helps insulate the bulk water💧 below and reducing the water thickness to be heated.
  • Top layer : A hydrophobic, porous layer allows water vapour to escape efficiently while heat loss is minimized

  • Bottom layer : A hydrophillic, porous material pulls water up, ensuing a constant, thin supply of salt water🧂🌊 to the hot upper layer

  • Effect : Salt ions are forced to remain in the bulk water or precipitate only on the edges, rather than blocking the porous structure.
